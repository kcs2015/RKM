Project: New Book Author Website

Status: In Development
Client: Book Author
Project Type: New Wordpress Website

Objectives:

  • Must incorporate media assets from latest book
  • Must be optimized for all devices (Mobile Responsive)
  • Must be easy to update and have blogging platform (Wordpress)
  • Must incorporate colors of latest book cover across website

Technology Used:

The Results:

This project allowed us to use our imagination while still incorporating our clients ideas into the design. After such a collaborative effort our result will be a fantastic website that:

  • Builds reader loyalty by:
    • Creating a truly memorable experience with the use of custom fonts, consistent imagery (the trail of hearts) and colors
    • Drives visitors to experience our author in various ways.
    • Allows visitors to interact with many of the author's work with an easy to navigate layout
  • Builds social proof by strategically showcasing their most compelling reader reviews
  • Increase book sales by guiding visitors through the website and eventually to their book purchasing page
