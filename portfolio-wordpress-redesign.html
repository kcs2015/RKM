Project: Mobile Responsive Wordpress Redesign

Status: In Development
Client: Moving Company
Project Type: Website Redesign

Objectives:

  • Must be mobile responsive
  • Must be setup using Wordpress
  • Provide clean easy to navigate layout
  • Maintain brand colors across website
  • Build a multipage form for quote requests

Technology Used:

The Results:

Our client started this process with a dated website that was created many years ago. Their website became such an "eye-soar" they were afraid of sending potential customers and clients there at all costs.

After working with us they not only will have a very clean, modern and most important mobile responsive website that will:

  • Increase their conversions by:
    • Streamlining their many quote requests with an interactive multi page form
    • Providing helpful content to their current and prospective customers through their Wordpress blog
    • Clearly and elegantly displaying thier prices & rates with Pricing Tables
    • Showcasing some of their most compelling testimonials
  • Build their customer loyalty by:
    • Becoming a resource center for their current customers
  • Buiding exposure to their social media accounts with strategic link placement on their website
  • Increased their credibility with a logo slider of their hard earnered partnerships
  • Increase productivity by reducing time spent on repetitve questions with an easy to navigate FAQ section
  • Increased their search engine ranking potential (non-mobile responsive websites will be penalized by Google)
